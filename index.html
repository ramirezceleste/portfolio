About me


Hi there, I'm Celeste. I'm a full-stack web developer living in sunny San Diego, CA. I recently graduated from a Coding Bootcamp and am currently a full-time student at Washington State University Global Campus. Learning code has completely changed my life. Sounds silly, I know but honestly, it has quickly become a passion of mine. I love the challenge, the learning process, creating beautifully designed, responsive and user-friendly websites. Aside from coding, I have a personal lifestyle blog which you can check out if you are interested here. Drinking coffee, going on long walks with my dog, watching Game of Thrones (can it be 2019 already?!), photography and trying not to buy everything in the "Home" section at Target are just some quick facts about me.
