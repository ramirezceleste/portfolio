Project AIDS Nigeria

Project AIDS Nigeria (PAN) is organized exclusively for the charitable and educational purpose of helping rural communities in Nigeria combat the HIV/AIDS epidemic. For my last project during Coding Bootcamp my group and I got an amazing opportunity to re-design and add more responsive elements to an existing website. Our client and founder of PAN, Domale, wanted a more modern website with an interactive curriculum and a way for students to get certified to start a PAN club at their school. We made sure to pay attention to details to ensure our client would be satisfied with the ending result.
